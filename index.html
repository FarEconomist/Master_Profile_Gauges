About Us



Master Profile Gauges is a ‘Splined and Relation Gauges’ manufacturing small scale Industry established in 2016, in Aurangabad. Industry’s specialization is in manufacturing of the spline gauges (for gear/involute shaped components checking) and relation gauges (for the checking of the various specifications of a component like shaft bores having flange with holes for fastening or other purposes.

These gauges are specially designed to check the positions and relations between such various product specifications). The industry has received ‘ISO 9001:2015’ certification for its quality management and quality assurance policies for consistent product quality and customer satisfaction. The Industry also received the awards from Marathwada Association of Small Scale Industries & Agriculture (MASSIA) for its excellence in the field of gauges manufacturing.
